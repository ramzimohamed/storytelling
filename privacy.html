This Privacy Policy explains how Story Telling ("the Service"), operated by [YOUR NAME / OPERATOR] ("we", "us"), handles data. The Service is an automation tool that publishes the operator's own AI-generated videos to the operator's own TikTok account. It is not a consumer product and does not collect data from third-party end users.

1. Data we process

  • TikTok authorization data: OAuth access and refresh tokens, and the basic account identifier (via user.info.basic), obtained when the account owner authorizes the Service.
  • Content metadata: the generated videos, their captions, and publishing status.

2. Purpose and legal basis

Data is processed for the sole purpose of publishing the operator's own content to the operator's own TikTok account. Where the GDPR applies, the legal basis is the legitimate interest of the operator in running their own publishing tool.

3. How data is used

TikTok tokens are used only to authenticate with TikTok and to publish content on the operator's behalf. We do not sell, rent, or share authorization data with any party other than TikTok as required to provide the Service.

4. Third-party providers

The Service interacts with TikTok (for publishing) and with AI providers used to generate content. These providers process data under their own privacy policies.

5. Storage and security

Authorization tokens are stored securely and are not exposed publicly. Access is limited to the operator.

6. Retention

Authorization data is retained only as long as the TikTok account remains connected to the Service and is deleted when access is revoked.

7. Your rights

Where applicable law (including the GDPR) grants rights to access, correct, or delete personal data, those rights can be exercised by contacting us. The account owner may revoke the Service's access at any time from their TikTok account settings.
