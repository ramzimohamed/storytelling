Story Telling

A tool that creates and publishes original, AI-generated short videos to the operator's own TikTok account.

What it does

Story Telling generates original short-form videos — script, voiceover, and visuals — and publishes them to the operator's own TikTok account through TikTok's official Content Posting API. It is an automation tool operated by and for its owner; it does not act on behalf of third parties.

AI-generated content

All videos produced by Story Telling are AI-generated and are flagged as AI-generated content (AIGC) before they are published, in line with TikTok's transparency requirements. The tool never reposts or republishes third-party content.

How it connects to TikTok

  • The account owner authorizes the app with their own TikTok account via OAuth (Login Kit).
  • Videos are published directly to the owner's profile via Direct Post (Content Posting API).
  • Posting is rate-limited; the owner controls what is published.
